Welcome

From one counter to a thousand shelves

A short note on who we are and how this catalogue works.

Sai Baba Traders is a Hyderabad-based super stockist and large-scale distributor, supplying retailers, kirana stores, supermarkets and sub-distributors across the region with stock from twelve of the country's most trusted FMCG manufacturers.

This catalogue is organised the way our godown is organised — by category, then by principal company — so that whether you stock a general store, a medical counter or a supermarket aisle, you can find your order sheet quickly. Every brand listed here is billed with full GST documentation, and every carton that leaves our warehouse is checked against the order before it goes out.

Pages ahead cover our infrastructure, our coverage area, our full product range by category, how wholesale ordering works, and our terms for retail and wholesale buyers. For anything not listed, our sales team maintains a live stock sheet — call the numbers on the back cover.

Infrastructure

Warehouse & godown operations

Stock handling is the backbone of a distribution business — here is how ours runs.

Storage & Handling

  • Segregated racking by principal company and category
  • FIFO (first-in first-out) stock rotation on every SKU
  • Dry, ventilated storage suited to food, cosmetic and pharma-adjacent items
  • Routine pest control and housekeeping schedule
  • Manual and register-based stock verification alongside billing records

  • Tie-ups with transport operators for outstation and pan-India dispatch

Coverage

Trusted pan-India, rooted in Hyderabad

Our home base is Attapur, Hyderabad — with dispatch reach well beyond it.

Our godown in Attapur is centrally placed for daily van delivery across Hyderabad and the surrounding districts of Telangana. For retailers and sub-stockists outside this radius, we book consignments through partner transport operators, giving us the ability to serve buyers across the country from a single warehouse.

Retail counters typically order in smaller, mixed quantities for daily shelf top-ups; wholesale buyers and sub-distributors order in full cartons or pallet lots for onward supply. We maintain separate order lines for both, listed on the contact page of this catalogue.

Order Lines by Type

  • Retail Orders — for shop-level, daily and mixed-carton orders
  • Wholesale Orders — for bulk, full-carton and sub-stockist orders
  • Both lines are staffed for order booking, stock checks and dispatch status

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do you supply outside Hyderabad?

Yes. Local orders are delivered by our own van; outstation and pan-India orders are dispatched through our transport partners.

Can I order a mix of brands in one call?

Yes — most retail orders combine several principal companies on a single invoice. Wholesale bulk orders are usually placed brand-wise for full-carton efficiency.

Which number should I call — retail or wholesale?

Use Retail Orders for shop-level, mixed-carton buying, and Wholesale Orders for bulk, full-carton or sub-stockist supply.

Do you provide GST invoices?

Yes, every order is billed against GSTIN 36AHPPJ1299L3ZM with a proper tax invoice and delivery challan.

Is this catalogue's product list complete?

It covers our standard range by category. For SKUs not listed, our order desk maintains a live stock sheet — please call to confirm availability.
